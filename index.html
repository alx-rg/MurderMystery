Murder Mystery Aboard the Ocean Liner

March 16th @ 5pm, come ready to discover the KILLER...

Guests on the HMS Hope

It's 1950, and something sinister is taking place onboard the maiden voyage of the HMS Hope.

Can we save this cruise from disaster and discover whodunnit?

Dress up and bring a suitable prop

We're on a cruise in the 1950's, the theme is nautical.

Please costume yourself according to your character's unique personality and occupation.

The award goes to...

- Best Detective
- Best Costume
- Most impressive acting

There'll be three awards. Be ready to play your character to the best of your abilities (for the most fun!)

The Plot

The plot is broken down into three main rounds, designed to run as one round over each course of a meal. There will be a short break at the end of each round so that we can make notes and discuss what's happened so far.

Quinn

Name: Inez Cent

Occupation: Aspiring Singer and Dancer

I've always dreamt of being a famous singer. I'll sing at any and every opportunity to showcase my wonderful talent. It's easy, I just sing about whatever's happening where I am. It's a real shame about Serena but she was getting terrribly old and talentless. I really respected her! I even threw a handful of pearls across the stage before her entrance.. as an homage. How was I to know she'd slip?
