Murder Mystery Aboard the Ocean Liner

Saturday March 16th @ 5pm,
Please bring a dish, it's a potluck!
Come ready to discover the KILLER...

Guests on the HMS Hope

It's 1950, and something sinister is taking place onboard the maiden voyage of the HMS Hope.

Can we save this cruise from disaster and discover whodunnit?

Dress up and bring a suitable prop

We're on a cruise in the 1950's, the theme is nautical.

Please costume yourself according to your character's unique personality and occupation.

The award goes to...

- Best Detective
- Best Costume
- Most impressive acting

There'll be three awards. Be ready to play your character to the best of your abilities (for the most fun!)

The Plot

The plot is broken down into three main rounds, designed to run as one round over each course of a meal. There will be a short break at the end of each round so that we can make notes and discuss what's happened so far.

Quinn

Name: Inez Cent

Occupation: Aspiring Singer and Dancer

I've always dreamt of being a famous singer. I'll sing at any and every opportunity to showcase my wonderful talent. It's easy, I just sing about whatever's happening where I am. It's a real shame about Serena but she was getting terrribly old and talentless. I really respected her! I even threw a handful of pearls across the stage before her entrance.. as an homage. How was I to know she'd slip?

Jacques

Name: Ahab Whalesworth

Occupation: Fisherman Turned Whale Enthusiast

Formerly a tycoon in the perfume trade, I used to hunt whales for their precious ambergris used in perfume manufacture. However, after a mysterious accident, when I was swept overboard and narrowly escaped death, I’m a changed man. I’m an eco-warrior and my passion for whales borders on the uncomfortable! When I’m nervous or uncertain, I sing whale songs.

Bronwyn

Name: Patsea Diver

Occupation: Jeweller

I believe diamonds are a girl's only real friend. I'd knock down my own grandma for a gem! When in public, I'm adorned from head to toe in bling, but if one of the unwashed masses even glances my way I transform into a feral racoon. I'll screech, hiss and claw at anyone who even looks at my precious jewels. It's unfortunate as I'm always showing them off too.

Corey

Name: Ace Berg

Occupation: Playboy Millionaire & Socialite

I’m the world’s most charming and handsome man (according to my mother). I am a master of the seductive arts, and never short of a pick up line. Some people say I’m spoiled, but they are just jealous poor people.

Christina

Name: Frida Floats

Occupation: Tarot card, reader, spiritualist, and clairvoyant

Nothing ever occurs without me knowing about it beforehand. Except in this case. I have the uncanny gift of perfect hindsight, and the rich and famous depend on me to guide their hearts and wallets. I am no phony, though, I just spoke to someone’s dead relative, and they agreed. If I didn’t like someone, then I’ll usually predict that they will die horribly. Sometimes, the spirits take control of me and voice their opinions through me. You can’t be mad at me for it though. The spirits said you were fat, not me.

Pat

Name: David Jones-Lockerton

Occupation: Ship Designer

I’ve designed and built the grandest vessels. The world has ever seen. These vessels and I have travelled the world carried loads of booty and seen many ports. I’m definitely not a pirate, although people say I act and sound like one. I like nothing more than singing, rousing sea shanties that I make up on the spot.

Renee

Name: Suz Spicious

Occupation: Society Pages Gossip Columnist

I believe the pen is mightier than the sword. It’s why I stabbed my husband with one when we got divorced. I also have a tongue that’s sharper than any knife. Truth is important to me and the greatest truth is that I’m better than everyone else! Don’t be offended if I ask you horribly offensive questions all evening, it’s just my job sweetie!

Alex

Name: Billy Ocean

Occupation: Safety Inspector of the HMS Hope

You are the safety inspector of the HMS hope, which is the most important job in the world. You have a knack for scrutinizing details and maintaining safety at any cost. All guests on your ship are a potential hazard, and must be treated as such to save them for potential disaster. It would be a stretch for people to call you a hero but you don’t think so!
